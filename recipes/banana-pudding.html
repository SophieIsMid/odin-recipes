Banana Pudding

Description

This is one of my favorite desserts! It is very simple and quick to make. and the best part is that it requires no baking!.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cold milk
  • 1 (5oz.) package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 1 (14oz.) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 (12oz.) container frozen whipped topping, thawed (make sure to use frozen whipped topping, canned whipped cream does not have the same consistency!)
  • 1 (16oz.) package vanilla wafers
  • 1 large bunch of bananas, sliced

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients; make sure your whipped topping is thawing, your milk is cold and your bananas are sliced.
  2. Place milk and pudding mix into a large bowl, and whisk until mix is fully dissolved.
  3. Whisk in the condensed milk until smooth.
  4. Stir in the vanilla, and fold the whipped topping into the mixture.
  5. Layer in your dish wafers, banana slices, and pudding mixture. Create multiple layers if you have a taller dish.
  6. Chill, and top with crushed wafers when ready to serve.
